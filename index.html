Keep the familiar structure

Top-level folders, subfolders, and bookmark links stay exactly where you already expect them to be. This is not a new system to learn.

Use the sidebar as a working surface

Open folders vertically, drag links into place, and manage bookmarks without leaving the page you are on.

Explain it in one sentence

Sidebar Favorites is basically your Chrome bookmark bar, just moved into a cleaner side panel with more room to breathe.

How it works

The bookmarks do not change. The presentation does.

1

Open the extension from the Chrome toolbar.

2

See your bookmark folders and links in a vertical sidebar tree.

3

Open, edit, drag, and organize the same favorites without the cramped horizontal bar.
